The Ten Largest Cities of the United States
New York City, New York, United States

New York City, NY

New York City is the largest city in New York and in the United States. New York City has five different boroughs like Manhattan, the Bronx, Queens, Brooklyn and Staten Island. New York City is home to tourists and number of attractions like the Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, the Central Park,and many other places but last at not least, the Times Square. The amazing fact about New York City is the diversity where a lot of people living in America, actually immigrated from countries in European countries, India, Pakistan, and many other countries, plus 800 languages are spoken besides English. Sadly, New York city became a target for numerous occasions like thee September 11, 2001 attacks and Hurricane Sandy. But on a happier side, New York City remains the dream city where a city never sleeps.

Los Angeles,California,United States

Los Angeles,CA

Los Angeles is the second largest city in the United States, and the largest city in California.Los Angeles is home to tourists and numerous attractions compared to New York City, but in the West Coast. Los Angeles is home to Hollywood Bowl, Hollywood Sign, the movie and TV sets and beautiful beaches.Since Los Angeles is home to Hollywood, the entertainment industry,the bigger film production studios like Paramount Pictures, Warner Brothers and Universal Pictures offer tourists a behind-the scene tour, which is anyone's dream while touring Los Angeles. Los Angeles has the Mediterranean-like weather compared to New York City, as NYC has the coldest weather, here in Los Angeles and its surrounding metropolis cities in Southern California has the beautiful weather surrounded by mountains and Pacific Ocean.

Chicago,Illinois ,United States

Chicago,IL

Chicago is the largest city in Illinois and the third largest city in the United States. Chicago is known for its skyline punctuated skyscrapers, John Hancock Center and Willis Tower are one of them.The city of Chicago is also known for art museums, especially Art Institute of Chicago with its noted Impressionist and post-Impressionist works.Chicago is also home to numerous things like first U.S. blood bank, open-heart surgery, first televised presidential debate and it has only one river that flows backward, which is pretty interesting for those who live in Chicago or want to tour the city of Chicago.

Houston,Texas,United States

Houston,TX

Houston is the largest city in the state of Texas and the fourth largest city in the United States. Houston is home to numerous sports teams like Houston Astros, Houston Rockets, and Houston Texans,like any other cities in the United States is home to avid sport fans.Houston is known for world capital of space exploration,so Space Center Houston is the tourist attraction to visit and know about space exporation. The downtown Houston is known to Houston Grand Opera and Houston Symphony along with Miller Outdoor Theatre plus the malls especially Galleria Mall, which is a must try to visit places Houstonians and people who are here in Houston for first time.

Phoenix,Arizona,United States

Phoenix,AZ

Phoenix is the fifth largest city in the United States and the capital of Arizona.Arizona is known for year-round sun and warm temperature. Arizona leads the nation in copper production, which is the state's abundant mineral. Phoenix does not have daylight saving time.Phoenix sits with Sonoran Desert, which is home to saguaro cactus, this cactus can grow as tall as 50 feet and live long as 200 years.The prominent landmark in Phoenix is Camelback Mountain.Unfortunately, Phoenix is not known for its towering buildings in down Phoenix unlike big cities like Chicago, Los Angeles, Houston, and New York City, but the tallest building in Phoenix is 40 story Chase Towers building known for local financial heavyweight, Chase Bank.

Philadelphia,Pennsylvania,United States

Philadelphia,PA

Philadelphia is the largest city in Pennsylvania. Philadelphia is known for the food, Philly Cheesesteak sandwich.Amazingly,Philadelphia is the birthplace of the Declaration of Independence written by Thomas Jefferson during the American Revoluionary War along with being the birthplace of the United States of America. Philadelphia has numerous must-see attractions such as The Liberty Bell Center, Independence Visitor Center,Valley Forge Historical Park and Philadelphia Museum of Art.

San Antonio,Texas,United States

San Antonio,TX

San Antonio is the most visited city in Texas. Home to the Alamo, River Walk, Six Flags Fiesta Texas, sizeable military presence and SeaWorld.There is a tower that oversees San Antonio, Towers of America (750 feet).San Antonio was founded in 1718 when a Spanish expedition of missionaries and explorers established the Mission San Antonio de Valero.The first Church's Chicken restaurant opened in San Antonio.Plus,San Antonio is home to oldest church which is San Fernando Catehdral (1738) and remains the heart of Catholic religion in San Antonio.

San Diego,California,United States

San Diego,CA

San Diego is called "the birthplace of California".Known for its mild year-round climate,natural deep-water harbor, extensive beaches, long association with the United States Navy and last but not least, emergence of healthcare and biotechnology development center. San Diego is home to largest oceanographic museum, the Birch Aquarium at Scripps, in the USA.San Diego is the main producer of avocados.

Dallas,Texas,United States

Dallas,TX

Dallas is the most visited city in Texas. Home to the State Fair of Texas. Dallas is the ninth largest city in United States. Dallas is located in North Central Texas. In winter holiday season, the Galleria Mall in Dallas is home to the country's tallest indoor Christmas tree.Dallas,Texas is known for filming few movies and TV shows like "Born on the Fourth of July"; "RoboCop"; "Walker,Texas Ranger". Interestingly, Dallas has a public library that is known to permanently display one of the original copies of the Declaration of Independence.The first convenience store, 7-eleven started in Dallas, even the corporation headquarter is there today. The Dallas area is the largest metropolitan area in the nation not surrounded by navigable body of water.

San Jose,California,United States

San Jose,CA

San Jose is called "Capital of Silicon Valley".Known for its Mediterranean climate, extremely high cost of living and the location of San Jose is within the booming high tech industry, as cultural, political and economic center. The third largest city in California and tenth largest city in the United States.San Jose and rest of Silicon Valley made contribution to California's fastest-growing economy.Before the Silicon Valley era, San Jose was established as an agricultural center for Spanish colony of Nueva California, later called as Alta California.Number of famous people like Steve Wozniak, co-founder of Apple Computers; Kate Walsh, actress and many other people are from San Jose,CA.The oldest church in California, located in San Jose is Cathedral Basilica of St.Joseph.
